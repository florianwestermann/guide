## Intent
Use motion to explain change, not to decorate it.

## Guidelines
- Motion should clarify state changes (open/close, reorder, navigation, feedback).
- Keep durations short and consistent; avoid stacked/competing animations.
- Prefer easing that feels responsive; avoid overshoot unless it communicates physics/weight.
- Always support reduced motion (honor OS/user preference and provide fallbacks).
